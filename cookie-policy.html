Cookie Policy

I value your privacy. This page explains how cookies are used on this portfolio website to enhance your experience and analyze site traffic via Google Analytics 4.

cookie

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and can be used to improve your experience on future visits.

info

Types of Cookies

Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
First-Party Cookies: Set by the website you're visiting.
Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services (like analytics providers).

How This Website Uses Cookies

This portfolio website uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as remembering your cookie consent preferences and theme settings (dark/light mode). Without these cookies, certain features of the website would not work correctly.

  • cookie_consent: Remembers your cookie preferences so we don't ask you every time you visit.
  • theme: Stores your preferred color scheme (dark or light mode).

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help me understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This data helps me improve the website's content and user experience.

Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

This website uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. GA4 helps me understand how visitors use this portfolio, including:

  • Number of visitors and page views
  • How visitors found the website (search engines, social media, direct visits)
  • Which pages are most popular
  • How long visitors spend on the site
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type and browser used
